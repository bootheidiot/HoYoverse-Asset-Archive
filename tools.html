Simple Resolution Changer People-11

A simple tool for changing the resolution of windows programs in this case HoYo Games. Very helpful for getting wallpapers in their full resolutions when capturing art. I recommend recording in a wider resolution then is necessary for any event you are capturing as you can always crop it to your desired resolution later and this way you ensure you are capturing all of the art. Big thanks to People11 for making this. 🙂
